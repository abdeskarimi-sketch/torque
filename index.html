A · Valuation metrics

On-chain cost-basis measures — do they make the cycle's "deep value at the trough" claim, or refute it?

MVRV Ratio

What Whether holders, in aggregate, sit in profit or loss versus what they actually paid.
MVRV = Market Cap / Realized Cap Market Cap = price × circulating supply Realized Cap = Σ (value of each coin at the price it last moved on-chain)
Read >1 = average holder in profit; <1 = underwater. Historically >3.5–3.7 marks euphoric tops, <1 marks capitulation bottoms.
Role The cost-basis sanity check on the cycle. Current ~1.1 = accumulation zone, not yet capitulation.

MVRV Z-Score

What How statistically stretched MVRV is, so tops/bottoms compare across cycles on one scale.
Z = (Market Cap − Realized Cap) / σ(Market Cap) σ = standard deviation of market cap over history (variant: Z = (MVRV − mean₁₂ₘ) / std₁₂ₘ — 12-month rolling)
Read >+7 = extreme overvaluation (every prior top); deeply negative = undervaluation.
Role Normalizes MVRV. Current ~−2.4 = deep value, longest stretch below −2σ since the 2022 bottom.

B · Flow & positioning metrics

Who is buying, how leveraged they are, and where capital is rotating.

ETF net flows

What Net institutional demand through US spot Bitcoin ETFs (IBIT, FBTC, ARKB, …).
Net flow = Σ (shares created − shares redeemed) × NAV (per day / week)
Read Inflow = ETFs absorbing BTC (demand); outflow = ETFs shedding BTC (supply pressure).
Role Proxy for the marginal institutional bid. Currently negative (6+ weeks, −$3.1B YTD) → the down-leg isn't over.

BTC dominance

What Bitcoin's share of total crypto market value.
Dominance = Market Cap(BTC) / Market Cap(all crypto) × 100%
Read Rising = capital prefers BTC; falling below ~50% = altseason rotation into alts.
Role Decides BTC-vs-alt weighting. ~56% and rising = "internal selection," alts lagging.

Funding rate

What The recurring payment that keeps perpetual-futures price tethered to spot — a leverage gauge.
funding ≈ premium(perp − spot), paid ~every 8h positive → longs pay shorts (crowded longs) negative → shorts pay longs (crowded shorts)
Read High positive = leverage froth (top risk); neutral/negative after a flush = deleveraged.
Role Froth detector. Post-$861M long liquidation it's reset → no froth → not top-like.

Fear & Greed Index

What A 0–100 composite of market sentiment.
Composite of: volatility · momentum/volume · social media · dominance · search trends 0 = Extreme Fear · 100 = Extreme Greed
Read Contrarian at extremes — deep fear clusters near bottoms, greed near tops.
Role Sentiment contrarian. Extreme Fear now = accumulation-friendly backdrop.

C · Projection mechanics

How the trough date, peak date, and the price fan are actually derived.

Cycle leg durations (the backbone)

What The cycle split into two measured legs whose lengths have been remarkably stable.
Top→Bottom leg = days(peak → trough) past: 410 / 363 / 376 Bottom→Top leg = days(trough → next peak) past: 1067 / 1061 / 1050 Trough = 6 Oct 2025 + 360–410 d → Oct–Nov 2026 Peak = trough + 1050–1070 d → ~Q3 2029
Role Generates the dates — the model's highest-confidence output (timing, not price).

P10 / P50 / P90 fan

What The outcome range, replacing single point targets.
P50 = central path (mid trough, base peak) P90 = shallower trough + higher peak (optimistic) P10 = deeper trough + lower peak (pessimistic) trough price = 60–70% drawdown off the $126.3k ATH peak price = current × collapsing low-to-high multiple (~2.5–4×)
Caveat A scenario fan, not a statistically estimated distribution — labeled as percentiles for sizing convenience. Band width = honesty about uncertainty.

Amplitude decay

What The measured shrinkage of each cycle's size — why the 2029 peak cone is wide and modest.
peak-to-peak multiple = peakₙ / peakₙ₋₁ 3.5× → 1.8× low-to-high multiple = peakₙ / troughₙ₋₁ 130× → 20× → 8× drawdown = (peak − trough) / peak 94% → 77% → ~52%
Role Sets the diminishing peak target and the shallower-trough expectation.

D · Sizing & kill logic

Inverse-dispersion weighting

What Converts each asset's uncertainty into a position size — wider cone, smaller size.
Dispersion Dᵢ = P90_peak / P10_peak Weight wᵢ = (1 / Dᵢ) / Σ (1 / Dⱼ) (normalized to 100%)
Read An inverse-variance-style heuristic: higher conviction (tighter cone) earns more capital. BTC (1.68×) tightest → biggest weight; XRP (6.5×) widest → smallest.
